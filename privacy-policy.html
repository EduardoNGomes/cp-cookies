Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the CP Cookies extension collects, uses, and protects user information. We are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring that your information is handled securely.

1. Data Collection

The CP Cookies extension does not collect personal information or sensitive user data...

2. Use of Permissions

The permissions requested by the extension are used exclusively for its functionality, as described below:

  • Cookies: Access to cookies to copy, save, and reuse them across tabs, allowing developers to manage cookies efficiently.
  • Storage: Local storage of copied cookies so they can be reused later.
  • Tabs: Access to basic information about the active tab (such as the URL) to ensure the proper functioning of the extension on the appropriate domain.
  • Host: Permission to interact with HTTP/HTTPS pages, ensuring the extension's functionality on compatible websites.

These permissions are strictly used on the user's device and never for collecting or transmitting data to external servers.

3. Data Sharing

No information collected or accessed by the extension is shared with third parties. The extension operates exclusively on the user's device and does not transmit data over the internet.

4. Data Security

We take measures to protect the information accessed by the extension, ensuring it is used only as needed for its functionality. Locally stored data is protected by the browser's security mechanisms.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we recommend that users review the policy regularly.
